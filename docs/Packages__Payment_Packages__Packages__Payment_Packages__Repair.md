# Payments Repair

Updated On 12 April 2026 |
 1 Min(s) read

Feedback
Summarize

Payments Repair bundle (PMT-TPRE-PREM) provides payment repair and enrichment capabilities to augment straight through processing (STP).

Payments Repair Bundle includes:

- Operational repair and enrichment of payment messages to improve STP
- Configurable repair rules and reference data to address data quality issues
- Validation to help ensure payments meet scheme and correspondent requirements before routing/settlement

## Modules Included

The Payments Repair bundle (PMT-TPRE-PREM) includes the following modules:

| --- | --- |
