# Tasks for MASAV Clearing

Updated On 07 August 2023 |
 3 Min(s) read

Feedback
Summarize

MASAV accepts debit and credit instructions from banks on every working day. It clears the payments and sends the feedback file to the bank for confirmation. MASAV Credit Transfers (MASAVCT) are supported by MASAV clearing where Originating bank generates MASAVCT in response to a credit transfer initiation (MASAVCTI).

## Workflow

This section allows the user to perform the below activities:

[Initiating MASAV Credit Transfer](#)

MASAVCTI payments are initiated through Payment Order Application (POA) based on the inputs provided by the user.

To initiate a MASAVCTI transaction, follow the below steps:

1. Log in as **PAYUSER1** > **Payment Order** > **Input Payment Order** > **Israel** > **MASAV Credit transfer**.
2. Enter values in the following fields:
   - *Debit Account Number*
   - *Ordering OT ID Type*
   - *Institution ID*
   - *Institution Name*
   - *Payment Currency*
   - *Payment Amount*
   - *Beneficiary ID*
   - *Payment Execution Date*
3. Click the **Validate** icon to check for errors and overrides.
4. Click the **Commit** icon.

The transaction is initiated and sent for authorisation.

[Authorising, Viewing or Deleting MASAV Credit Transfer](#)

Payment initiated using POA must be authorised to proceed further.

To authorise a transaction, follow the below steps:

1. Log in as **PAYUSER2** > **Payment Order Menu**> **Authorise/Delete Payment Order** > **Authorise/Delete Payment Order**.
2. In the Payment Order Unauthorised List screen, click the **Authorise** icon.
3. In the Payment Order New screen, verify the details and click the **Authorise** icon.

The transaction is authorised.

To delete a transaction, follow the below steps:

1. Log in as **PAYUSER2** > **Payment Order Menu** > **Authorise/Delete Payment Order** > **Authorise/Delete Payment Order**.
2. In the Payment Order Unauthorised List screen, click the **Delete** icon corresponding to a record.
3. In the Payment Order New screen, click the **Delete** icon.

The transaction is deleted.

To view a transaction, follow the below steps:

1. Log in as **PAYUSER2** > **Payment Order Menu** > **Authorise/Delete Payment Order** > **Authorise/Delete Payment Order**.
2. In the Payment Order Unauthorised List screen, click the **View** icon corresponding to a record.

The Payment Order New screen displays the record in view mode.
