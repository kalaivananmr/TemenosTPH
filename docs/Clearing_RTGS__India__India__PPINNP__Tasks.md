The following tasks are available for the NEFT Clearing module.

## Workflow

In NEFT Clearing, the user can perform the following activities:

[Initiating an Outward National Electronic Fund Transfer - NEFT Request](#)

A national electronic fund transfer request is initiated through the Payment Order Screen based on the input by user.

1. Navigate to the **PAYUSER1 > Payment Order > Input Payment Order > India > National Electronic Fund Transfer - NEFT** menu to access the PAYMENT.ORDER,INDIA.NEFT version.
2. Fill in the details in the following fields: *Debit Account No*, *Payment Currency*, *Payment Amount*, *Beneficiary Name*, *Beneficiary Account No*, *Beneficiary Bank Ifsc Code*.
3. Click on the **Validate** icon to check for errors and overrides.
4. Click on the **Commit** icon.
5. The transaction is completed if no error message displayed.

[Authorising a National Electronic Fund Transfer - NEFT](#)

The payments initiated through the Payment Order screen need to be approved for further processing.

To authorise a transaction, follow the steps below.

1. Navigate to the **PAYUSER2 > Payment Order > Authorise/Delete Payment Order > Authorise/Delete Payment Order** menu.
2. Click on the **Authorise** icon.
3. In the Authorise screen, verify the details and then click on the **Authorise** icon.
